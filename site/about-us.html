Who We Are

Founded in 2014 by Nosa and Ruth Omoregie

Sisters Heart Consult is an Ireland-based event planning company with a mission that extends beyond just organizing spectacular events. While we offer expert event planning services to a diverse clientele, we also proactively organize a variety of signature events, such as teen summer camps, women's nights out, and inclusive fundraising galas.

The funds raised from these events are pivotal to the success of our Sisters' Heart Initiative, which dedicates resources to support widows and children in Nigeria and other African countries. This initiative not only provides immediate assistance but also works towards sustainable empowerment of these communities.
